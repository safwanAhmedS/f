About Us

Blooming Petals is one of the finest floral designers, specialized in a variety of beautiful, unique and original floral designs for weddings, parties, banquets, corporate events, inaugurations & more. We are also specialized in professional flower arrangements based on individual client requirements. We provide the most stunning, unique creations for almost every type of event.
